## 1. Executive Summary

Meridian Financial Services is evaluating the Meridian Automated Loan Underwriting System, a high-risk AI system that uses the CrediSure Credit Decision Engine to support automated small business loan underwriting. The system is designed to process approximately **94% of loan applications automatically**, with approximately **6% routed to human review**.

This risk register identifies six priority AI risks:

| # | Risk |
|---|---|
| **Risk 1** | Discriminatory lending outcomes |
| **Risk 2** | Proxy bias through credit and business variables |
| **Risk 3** | Weak explainability and incomplete reason codes |
| **Risk 4** | Accountability gaps in third-party AI deployment |
| **Risk 5** | Inaccurate automated denials |
| **Risk 6** | Lack of meaningful human oversight |

**Current Risk Position:** The system must not receive full production approval until required mitigations, evidence, ownership, monitoring, and governance approvals are completed.

### Risk 1 — Discriminatory Lending Outcomes

| | |
|---|---|
| **Risk Source** | IBM AI Risk Atlas / IBM AI Risk Database |
| **IBM Risk Theme** | Discriminatory actions |
| **Risk Owner** | Compliance Reviewer |
| **Approver** | Executive Approver |
| **AI Lifecycle Phase** | Deployment / Operation |
| **Applicable Frameworks** | EU AI Act · NIST AI RMF · ISO/IEC 42001 |

**Risk Description**

The AI underwriting system may produce unfair approval or denial outcomes for protected or vulnerable groups due to biased training data, proxy variables, or historical lending patterns. Even if protected characteristics are not directly used, variables such as geography, industry, business age, thin credit file, and cash-flow volatility may create discriminatory effects.

**Risk Scoring**

| | |
|---|---|
| **Inherent Likelihood** | Possible |
| **Inherent Severity** | Critical |
| **Current Risk Level** | **Very High** |
| **Mitigation Status** | In Progress |
| **Residual Likelihood** | Unlikely |
| **Residual Severity** | Major |
| **Residual Risk Level** | **Medium / High** |

**Key Mitigation**

Require pre-production fairness testing, disparate impact analysis, segment-level approval and denial monitoring, validation of denial reason codes, and governance committee review before launch.

> **Recommendation:** Do not approve production until fairness testing and monitoring controls are documented and reviewed.
