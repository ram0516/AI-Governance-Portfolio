# 11 — ISO/IEC 42001 Assessment

**Framework:** ISO/IEC 42001 — Artificial Intelligence Management System  
**Platform:** [VerifyWise](https://cyberpros.verifywise.ai/)  
**System:** Meridian Automated Loan Underwriting System

## Summary

Assessment of Meridian Financial Services against ISO/IEC 42001 clauses and annexes as part of the AI governance review for the automated loan underwriting system.

## Clauses Assessed

| Clause | Title | Status |
|---|---|---|
| 4 | Context of the Organization | In Review |
| 5 | Leadership | In Review |
| 6 | Planning | In Review |
| 7 | Support | In Review |
| 8 | Operation | In Review |
| 9 | Performance Evaluation | In Review |
| 10 | Improvement | In Review |

## Annexes Assessed

| Annex | Title | Status |
|---|---|---|
| A | Reference Control Objectives and Controls | In Review |
| B | Guidance for Implementing AI Controls | In Review |

## Key Findings

- Meridian has no formal AI Management System (AIMS) in place prior to this assessment
- Leadership accountability for AI governance is not yet formally assigned
- AI risk management process is being established as part of this governance exercise
- Monitoring and continual improvement procedures require formalization before production deployment
