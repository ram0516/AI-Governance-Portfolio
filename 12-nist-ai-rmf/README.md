# 12 — NIST AI RMF Assessment

**Framework:** NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF 1.0)  
**Platform:** [VerifyWise](https://cyberpros.verifywise.ai/)  
**System:** Meridian Automated Loan Underwriting System

## Summary

Assessment of the Meridian Automated Loan Underwriting System against NIST AI RMF core functions.

## Core Functions

| Function | Description | Status |
|---|---|---|
| **GOVERN** | Policies, processes, procedures, and practices for AI risk management | In Review |
| **MAP** | AI risks identified and categorized in organizational context | In Review |
| **MEASURE** | AI risks analyzed, prioritized, and tracked | In Review |
| **MANAGE** | AI risks addressed with appropriate responses and mitigations | In Review |

## Key Findings

**GOVERN** — Meridian lacks a formal AI governance policy and ownership structure. Established as part of this assessment through the policy manager and training registry.

**MAP** — Risk mapping completed via the six-risk risk register, incorporating IBM AI Risk Database and MIT AI Risk Repository sources alongside custom risks.

**MEASURE** — Inherent and residual risk levels assessed for all six identified risks. Risk 6 (insufficient human oversight at 94% automation) is rated highest: Catastrophic / Almost Certain.

**MANAGE** — Mitigations defined per risk; several remain open pending vendor documentation, fairness testing, and human oversight procedure formalization.
